We choose and rank Pokémon based on the following criteria:
  • If the Pokémon takes reduced damage or is immune to damage from one of the base or Tera types of the raid Pokémon
  • If the raid Pokémon's Tera type overlaps with its base types (for increased Same Type Attack Bonus, or STAB)
  • If the Pokémon has a type where it could benefit from STAB and the move would be super effective against the raid Pokémon
  • The Pokémon does not have a type that is weak to the raid Pokémon

Pokémon and types are ranked from most (red glow) to least (green glow) recommended.
